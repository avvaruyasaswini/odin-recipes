Vegetable Fried Rice

Description

Vegetable fried rice is a quick and satisfying dish made by stir-frying cooked rice with vegetables, sauces,
and simple seasonings. It's a great way to use leftover rice and can be customized with whatever vegetables
you have at home.

Ingredients

  • Cooked rice
  • oil
  • Garlic
  • Onion
  • Carrot
  • Beans or peas
  • Soy sauce
  • Salt
  • Black Pepper
  • Spring Onions (optional)

Steps

  1. Heat oil in a pan over medium heat.
  2. Add garlic and onion and saut√© until fragrant.
  3. Add chopped vegetables and cook until slightly tender.
  4. Add the cooked rice and mix well.
  5. Add soy sauce, salt, and pepper and stir until evenly combined.
  6. Garnish with spring onions and serve hot.
