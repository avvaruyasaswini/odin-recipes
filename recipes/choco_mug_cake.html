Chocolate Mug Cake

Description

Chocolate mug cake is a quick dessert made in a single mug using simple ingredients.
It's soft, chocolatey, and perfect when you want something sweet without baking an entire cake.

Ingredients

  • All-purpose flour
  • Sugar
  • Cocoa powder
  • Baking powder
  • Milk
  • Oil
  • Vanilla essence (optional)
  • Chocolate chips (optional)

Steps

  1. Add all the dry ingredients to a mug and mix well.
  2. Pour in the milk, oil, and vanilla essence and stir until smooth.
  3. Add chocolate chips if using.
  4. Microwave the mug for about 1-2 minutes until the cake is set.
  5. Let it cool slightly and enjoy.
