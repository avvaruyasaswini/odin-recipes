Garlic Noodles

Description

Garlic noodles are a quick and flavorful dish made by tossing cooked noodles with fragrant garlic,
butter or oil, and simple seasonings. They’re comforting, slightly spicy if you want them to be,
and perfect when you want something satisfying without spending hours in the kitchen.

This dish is very flexible. You can keep it plain, or add vegetables, sauces, or protein depending on your mood.
The star of the dish is the garlic, golden and aromatic, coating every strand of noodle.

Ingredients

  • Noodles (any kind)
  • Garlic (finely chopped)
  • Oil or Butter
  • Soy Sauce
  • Chilli sauce or chilli flakes
  • Black Pepper
  • Salt
  • Spring Onions (optional)

Steps

  1. Boil the noodles according to the package instructions and drain them.
  2. Heat oil or butter in a pan and add the chopped garlic.
  3. Sauté the garlic until it turns light golden and fragrant.
  4. Add soy sauce, chilli sauce, pepper, and salt, and mix well.
  5. Add the cooked noodles to the pan and toss until evenly coated.
  6. Garnish with spring onions and serve hot.
